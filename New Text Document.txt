# Project RealWork

**Environment:** Built in Google Antigravity
**Mission:** Automate the California False Claims Act (CFCA) oversight.
**Objective:** Identify, expose, and legally challenge government fraud, waste, and corporate hypocrisy using autonomous AI agents.
**Endgame:** Secure whistleblower payouts under seal, while generating packaged "Hit Kits" for independent journalists to force public accountability.

## 2. The Core Architecture

RealWork decouples the development environment from the production data pipeline to minimize runtime overhead and operational costs.

* **Development IDE:** Google Antigravity (Powered by Gemini 3.5 Flash / 3.1 Pro for workspace orchestration)
* **Pipeline Inference Engine:** Gemma (Lightweight open model optimized for high-throughput, low-cost structural data classification)
* **Backend:** Firebase
* **Data Ingestion (Ground Truth):** California Open Data Portal (Free state APIs)
* **Data Validation (Reality Check):** Bright Data Scraping Proxies (Hackathon Sponsor Requirement)

---

## 3. The Target Vectors

Depending on the data schema, the pipeline routes targets to one of two forensic analysis tracks:

### Track A: Ghost Projects (Infrastructure/Physical)
* **The Fraud:** Entities taking money for physical projects without doing the work.
* **The Ground Truth:** CA Grants Portal (High-dollar physical/environmental grants).
* **The Reality Check:** Scrape Google Maps and LinkedIn via Bright Data. Flag multi-million dollar recipients with residential addresses, empty lots, and zero employees.

### Track B: Executive Bloat (Homelessness/Services)
* **The Fraud:** Non-profits taking service/housing grants and masking the funds in overhead and executive salary spikes.
* **The Ground Truth:** CA Grants Portal (Homelessness and outreach grants).
* **The Reality Check:** Scrape ProPublica API / Form 990 tax returns via Bright Data. Flag entities where state funding increases perfectly correlate with sudden spikes in administrative overhead and CEO pay.

---

## 4. The Endgame: Strike Protocol

When a high-probability fraud is identified, RealWork executes a two-pronged strike.

### Route A: The Legal Strike (The Payout)
* Retain a *qui tam* attorney to file a lawsuit under the CFCA.
* **Status:** Filed *Under Seal*. Operators remain 100% anonymous. Successful recovery yields a 15% to 33% whistleblower payout.

### Route B: The Media Drop (The "Hit Kit")
* Generate a sterile "Hit Kit" (State receipt + Bright Data reality check + Gemma summary).
* **Action:** Securely and anonymously drop the Hit Kit to independent investigators to force an immediate public investigation.
